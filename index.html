Arrow Escape — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026
Contact: arslanattangent@gmail.com

Arrow Escape ("the app", "we") is a casual puzzle game published by Arslan Shaukat. This policy explains what data the app handles, and how.

The short version

  • The app has no account system and never asks for your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • Your game progress (levels, stars, coins, settings) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded by us.
  • Ads, purchases and analytics are provided by third-party services that process limited technical data, described below.
  • Ads in Arrow Escape are requested in non-personalized mode.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of analytics and purchase data at any time — see the data deletion instructions. Requests are completed within 30 days.

Data stored on your device

Game progress, cosmetic purchases made with in-game coins, and settings are saved locally on your device. Deleting the app deletes this data. We have no copy of it and cannot restore it.

Third-party services

Google AdMob (advertising)

The app shows optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may process your device's advertising identifier, IP address (which implies coarse location), and basic device information. We request non-personalized ads only. You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings at any time. See Google's privacy policy and how Google uses ad data. Purchasing Remove Ads permanently disables interstitial ads; rewarded ads remain available only when you choose to watch them.

RevenueCat (purchases)

The one-time "Remove Ads" purchase is processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and your purchase history for this app so your purchase can be restored on reinstall. RevenueCat does not receive your name or payment details — those stay with Apple/Google. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Google Firebase (analytics & crash reporting)

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand aggregate gameplay (e.g. which levels are hardest) and to fix crashes. Firebase processes an app-instance identifier, device model and OS version, country (from IP), and gameplay events such as "level completed". These events contain no personal information. See Firebase's privacy documentation.

Children

Arrow Escape is a family-friendly game but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are served in non-personalized mode for all players.

Your choices and rights

  • Ad personalization: ads are already non-personalized; you can additionally reset or limit your advertising ID in iOS/Android settings.
  • Deletion: deleting the app removes all locally stored data. To request deletion of data held by the third-party services above in connection with the app, email arslanattangent@gmail.com and we will assist, or contact the services directly via the links above.
  • EU/UK (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA) residents: we do not sell personal information. You may exercise access/deletion rights via the contact email.

Offline play

The full game works offline. When offline, no ad, purchase or analytics data is transmitted.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
