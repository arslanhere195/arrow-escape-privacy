Arrow Escape — Data Deletion Requests

Applies to the app Arrow Escape: Snake Puzzle by Arslan Shaukat (sigmastack) · Contact: arslanattangent@gmail.com

How to request deletion of your data

  1. Email arslanattangent@gmail.com with the subject line "Arrow Escape data deletion request".
  2. If possible, include your device's advertising identifier (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads) so third-party records can be matched — the app has no accounts, so this is the only identifier that can link data to your device.
  3. We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Analytics data (gameplay events, crash logs) held in Google Firebase — deleted on request.
  • Purchase records held in RevenueCat (anonymous purchase history for the Remove Ads product) — deleted on request. Note: Apple/Google keep their own store transaction records under their policies.
  • Advertising data is processed by Google AdMob; you can reset or delete your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings, which unlinks past ad data from your device.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Game progress (levels, stars, coins, settings) exists only on your device — we never have a copy. Uninstalling the app deletes it immediately.
  • No other data is retained after a deletion request is completed. There is no additional retention period beyond the 30-day processing window.

You can request deletion whether or not you still have the app installed. Full details in our privacy policy.
